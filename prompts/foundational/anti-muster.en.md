AVOID THESE MISTAKES:
- Generic titles like "5 Tips for...", "Mindset Tips", "Success Starts in the Mind"
- Hooks that start with "Did you know...", "In today's world...", "Let me tell you..."
- A body that says the same thing 3 times in different words
- CTAs like: "What do you think?", "Drop a like", "Follow for more"
- Abstract philosophy with no concrete example
- Every sentence on its own line with a blank line between (= obvious AI pattern)
- One-word rhetorical questions like "The result?" or "The kicker?"
- Pretentious short fragments: "Innovation. Disruption. Transformation."
