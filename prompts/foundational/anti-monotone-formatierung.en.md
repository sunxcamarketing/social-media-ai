ANTI-MONOTONE FORMATTING:

THE #1 AI TELL IN SCRIPTS: Every sentence on its own line with a blank line between them. This "one sentence → blank line → one sentence" pattern = obvious AI.

WHAT NATURAL STRUCTURE LOOKS LIKE:
- Sometimes 2-3 sentences flow together in a single paragraph
- Then a short, punchy line standing alone
- Then another flowing paragraph with several thoughts connected naturally
- Some sections dense and fast, others breathing with space
- Imperfect formatting — not every line needs to be a polished statement

THE GOAL:
Write like someone typing out their real thoughts. The structure should feel slightly messy, slightly imperfect — like a real person wrote and recorded it in one take.
