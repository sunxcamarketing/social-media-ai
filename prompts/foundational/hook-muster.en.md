6 HOOK TYPES that reliably stop the thumb. Pick the one that fits the topic:

1. COUNTER-INTUITIVE — says the opposite of what the audience believes.
   "Most coaches destroy their clients without knowing it."
   "Posting more makes your profile worse. Here's why."
   Works because: Contradicting someone's belief forces attention.

2. IDENTITY TRIGGER — hits a self-perception of the target audience.
   "If you're reading this, you're probably already too late."
   "You're the guy who's still on his phone at 11pm wondering why nothing's growing."
   Works because: People stop when they recognize themselves.

3. SHOCK NUMBER — a number that can't possibly be true (but is).
   "87% of all coaches make less than $2,000 a month. Here's why."
   "I had $180k in debt at 23. Today I make $40k a month."
   Works because: Concrete numbers are more credible than claims.

4. SECRET — implies hidden knowledge no one else is sharing.
   "What nobody tells you about personal branding — until now."
   "No consultant will tell you this, because it would kill their business model."
   Works because: FOMO + exclusivity.

5. DIRECT PROVOCATION — attacks the audience or their beliefs.
   "You post every day and wonder why nothing grows? That's exactly your mistake."
   "Stop making content. First, you need a strategy."
   Works because: An attack creates an emotional reaction → engagement.

6. RESULT-FIRST — shows the ending before explaining the path.
   "From 0 to 50k followers in 90 days. No ad budget."
   "3 months. $0 ad budget. 200 inquiries. Here's how."
   Works because: The result instantly validates that watching more is worth it.

IMPORTANT: For every script, write hooks in at least 3 DIFFERENT types. Not the same type 3 times with different words.
