HOOK/RETAIN/REWARD FRAMEWORK:

## HOOK (First 1-3 seconds): Stop the scroll
Pick from: Personal experience, Provocative claim, Contrast, Concrete observation, Universal problem.
Use at least 2 elements: Relevance + Conflict/Surprise/Identification.
Instant comprehension: No context required, anyone gets it in 2 seconds.

Hook formulas that work:
- Personal experience: "I did X wrong for 3 years."
- Provocative claim: "Your coach is lying to you."
- Contrast truth: "Stop doing X. Start doing Y."
- Concrete observation: "[Specific situation] taught me [unexpected lesson]."
- Universal problem: "Why do you [annoying thing] when [better option] exists?"

## RETAIN (Middle): Force them to stay
3 methods: Lists, Steps, Stories.
Every sentence delivers ONE new piece of information.
Specific and concrete — no vague statements.
Progressive value delivery — it gets better, not worse.
Smooth context build: Each sentence builds on the previous one.

## REWARD (End): Deliver the payoff
Meet or exceed the expectation set by the hook.
Clear, actionable insight — not abstract.
Memorable takeaway that can be applied immediately.
Appropriate specificity — not too general, not too niche.
