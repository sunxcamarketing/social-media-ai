# CORE INSTAGRAM STORIES PRINCIPLES

## The 3 Most Important Elements
1. **Hook in the first frame**: The first story must stop them with a clear call-out + implicit promise. If the first frame is weak, the rest doesn't matter.
2. **Proof over claims**: Use screenshots, behind-the-scenes, small wins, reposts. Show "I really do this, I really can do this."
3. **One clear CTA per day**: Tell people exactly what to do ("Write me 'ENGINE' if you...", "Reply with 1/2/3", "Click the link for...")

## The 4 Story Pillars (use these daily)
- **Pain & Mistakes**: Point out what's not working, common errors, lies in the industry
- **Process & System**: Workflows, strategies, app/tool insights, how you build plans from data (demonstration content)
- **Proof & Cases**: Before/after results, testimonials, story reposts from customers, chat screenshots
- **Pitch & CTA**: Spots available, deadlines, conditions, reminders of what they'll get

## The 5-Frame Story Structure
- Frame 1: Hook + Call-out (target the specific audience)
- Frame 2: Pain/Problem (what frustrates them today)
- Frame 3: Mini-solution or process insight (one sentence + visual proof like screenshot)
- Frame 4: Proof (screenshot, quote, short video showing results)
- Frame 5: CTA (clear instruction with keyword for DMs)

Think of each story as a micro-ad: **Hook → Value/Proof → CTA**

## Content Discovery Method
- Build a hooks list from top-performing content (top 10% by views/replies)
- Use 70% of new stories starting with variations of proven hooks
- Recycle best-performing stories/reels from the last 7-30 days
- Pay attention to audience signals (polls, question stickers, high-reply content)

**GOAL: Generate 1-3 DMs per day from the target audience using keyword responses from stories.**
