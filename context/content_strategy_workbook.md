# Content Strategy — How I Build Social Media Strategies

**Be The Brand — Content Strategie Workbook**

---

## Why a Content Strategy?

Many people post on social media randomly. Today a reel, tomorrow a quote, the day after nothing. The content feels arbitrary and growth stalls.

A strong personal brand is not built through individual posts, but through a clear strategy.

A strategy ensures:
- You always know what you can post
- Your content supports your goals
- Your target audience immediately understands what you stand for

**The framework has exactly 3 building blocks:**

```
1. Content Pillars  →  2. Content Types  →  3. Content Formats
```

---

## Lesson 1: Why a Content Strategy?

When someone sees your account regularly, they should immediately understand:
- Who you help
- What you can do for them
- Why they should follow you

If only one type gets posted, the account quickly becomes one-dimensional and untrustworthy.

---

## Lesson 2: Content Pillars

Content Pillars are the **3–5 main topics** you post about regularly. They answer the question:

> *What should your account be known for?*

### Step 1 — Brainstorming
Before defining your pillars, collect topics first. Which topics fit your brand?

### Step 2 — Focus
Select the 3–5 most important topics you will speak about regularly.

**Rule:** Each pillar must directly connect to your offer, your expertise, or your dream customer's problems. No niche needs more than 5 pillars.

## Lesson 3: Content Types

Not every post has the same goal. A successful account combines different types of content because each type serves a different function.

Some posts:
- Build trust
- Show expertise
- Deliver value
- Generate reach

### The 5 Core Content Types

| Content Type | Goal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Authority** | Shows your expertise | Analysis of accounts, industry insights |
| **Story** | Builds trust | Your journey or experience, failures, turning points |
| **Social Proof** | Shows results | Client wins, testimonials, before/after |
| **Education** | Delivers value | Tips or tutorials, step-by-step, frameworks |
| **Opinion / Meinung** | Generates reach | Hot takes, market critique, unpopular truths |

> If you only post one type, your account becomes one-dimensional and unbelievable.

| Content Type | Goal | Best For |
|---|---|---|

| **Social Proof** | Trigger buying decisions | Converting followers into clients |

## Lesson 4: Content Formats

Content formats define **how** your content is presented. The same topic can be turned into multiple formats.

Different formats ensure:
- More attention
- More variety
- Better performance

### Core Formats

| Format | Description |
|---|---|
| **Face to Camera** | Direct address to camera, personal & authentic |
| **Voice Over** | Narration over footage or visuals, no face required |
| **Story Telling** | Narrative arc — setup, conflict, resolution |
| **Karussell / Carousel** | Multi-slide educational or list content |
| **Reaction** | Respond to or react on existing content |
| **Screen Recording** | Tutorial or walkthrough content |
| **Behind the Scenes** | Raw, unpolished look at process or daily life |
| **Educational** | Structured teaching content, frameworks, how-tos |

## Lesson 5: The Content Formula

Many people know which topics they want to talk about, but don't know how to turn them into concrete posts.

**The formula:**

```
CONTENT  =  PILLAR  +  TYPE  +  FORMAT
```

Every post consists of these 3 building blocks:
- **Pillar** — What is it about?
- **Type** — What goal does the post have?
- **Format** — How is the content presented?

### Example

**Topic:** Instagram Growth

| Pillar | Type | Format | Post |
|---|---|---|---|
| Social Media Strategy | Education | Face to Camera | "3 Fehler die dein Instagram Wachstum zerstören" |
| Social Media Strategy | Opinion | Reaction | "Warum 90% der Instagram Tipps falsch sind" |

## Lesson 6: Redaktionsplan (Editorial Calendar)

A weekly posting structure turns the strategy into a system. Assign a content type and format to every posting slot.

**Example Weekly Structure:**

| Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Authority | Education | Story | Social Proof | Opinion |
| Face to Camera | Carousel | Voice Over | Screenshot | Reaction |

**Build your first content ideas:** Combine your Pillars, Content Types and Formats.

## Complete Framework Summary

```
STEP 1 — Define 3–5 Content Pillars
          (What is your account known for?)

STEP 2 — Map Content Types to Goals
          (What should each post achieve?)

STEP 3 — Choose Formats per Platform
          (How is the content presented?)

STEP 4 — Apply the Formula
          CONTENT = PILLAR + TYPE + FORMAT

STEP 5 — Build the Editorial Calendar
          (Assign types and formats to each posting slot)
```

| Layer | Question | Options |
|---|---|---|
| Pillar | What topic? | 3–5 core themes from your niche |
| Type | What goal? | Authority, Story, Proof, Education, Opinion + extensions |
| Format | How presented? | Face-cam, Carousel, Voiceover, Reaction, etc. |
| Goal | What outcome? | Reach, Trust, Conversions, Engagement |
