# Business Info

## Organization Overview

Aysun runs Sun x ca — a social media agency for German-speaking entrepreneurs, creators, and personal brands worldwide (DACH + international). The core services are social media management, content strategy, profile scaling, monetization, and community building. She helps clients go viral, attract customers organically, and turn their social media into a real sales channel.

## Products / Services

- **Social Media Management & Strategy** — Done-for-you management and strategic consulting for Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube
- **Profile Scaling & Monetization** — Systematic organic growth, community building, and converting followers into paying customers
- **Million Dollar Virality System** — An AI-powered pipeline that scrapes competitor Reels, identifies the most viral ones, analyzes them with AI, and generates adapted video concepts for clients
- Clients include coaches, consultants, creators, personal brands, and entrepreneurs across the German-speaking world

## Key Context

- The system was previously built and running inside **n8n** (low-code automation platform)
- Now migrating to a standalone code-based implementation for more control, flexibility, and scalability
- External services used: Apify (scraping), Google Gemini (video analysis), Anthropic Claude (concept generation), Google Sheets (data storage in n8n version)
